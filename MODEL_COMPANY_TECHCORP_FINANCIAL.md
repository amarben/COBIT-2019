### Step 3: Governance Design

#### Governance System Principles

TechCorp's governance system is built on these foundational principles:

1. **Value-Driven:** All IT decisions must demonstrably support enterprise goals and deliver measurable business value
2. **Risk-Aware:** Balance innovation speed with appropriate risk management and compliance
3. **Stakeholder-Centric:** Transparent engagement with all stakeholders, understanding and addressing their needs
4. **Accountability:** Clear roles, responsibilities, and decision rights at all levels
5. **Agile Governance:** Lightweight, adaptive processes that enable rather than hinder business agility
6. **Data-Informed:** Evidence-based decision making using metrics, analytics, and objective assessment

#### Governance Structures

**IT Steering Committee**
- Chair: CEO
- Members: CFO, CRO, CIO, Business Unit Leaders
- Frequency: Monthly
- Responsibilities: Strategic direction, major investment approval, portfolio prioritization

**Technology Risk Committee**
- Chair: CRO
- Members: CIO, CISO, CCO (Chief Compliance Officer), External Risk Expert
- Frequency: Bi-weekly
- Responsibilities: Risk oversight, cybersecurity posture, regulatory compliance

**Architecture Review Board**
- Chair: Chief Architect
- Members: Domain Architects, Senior Engineers, Product Leaders
- Frequency: Weekly
- Responsibilities: Architecture standards, technology decisions, technical debt management

**Change Advisory Board**
- Chair: Change Manager
- Members: Operations, Development, Security, Business Representatives
- Frequency: Bi-weekly
- Responsibilities: Change approvals, deployment coordination, rollback decisions

**Portfolio Management Office (PMO)**
- Director: VP Portfolio Management
- Staff: Portfolio Analysts, Project Managers, Business Analysts
- Responsibilities: Portfolio optimization, benefits tracking, resource allocation
