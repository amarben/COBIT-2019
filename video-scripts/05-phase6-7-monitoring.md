### SECTION 6: Best Practices & Tips (1 minute)

**[Screen: Dashboard or summary view]**

"Let me share some best practices from successful COBIT implementations:

**1. Executive Sponsorship is Critical**
- CIO or CEO-level sponsor
- Active Board engagement
- Regular executive communication

**2. Start Small, Think Big**
- Don't try to implement all 40 objectives
- Focus on priorities from Goals Cascade
- Quick wins build momentum
- Scale over time

**3. Measure What Matters**
- Link metrics to business outcomes
- Use both lag and lead indicators
- Keep dashboards simple and actionable
- Review regularly

**4. Communicate Constantly**
- Benefits realization stories
- Success celebrations
- Transparent about challenges
- Multiple channels (newsletter, town halls, Board reports)

**5. Build Capability Over Time**
- Target Level 3 initially (established, standardized)
- Level 4-5 comes later (predictable, optimizing)
- Investment in training pays off
- Patience and persistence

**6. Use the Tools**
- This application tracks everything
- Export regularly (backups!)
- Use dashboards in governance meetings
- Keep data current

**7. Adapt and Evolve**
- COBIT is a framework, not a cookbook
- Tailor to your context
- Design factors guide customization
- Continuous improvement mindset

**8. Don't Go It Alone**
- Build a community
- External consultants for expertise
- Peer networking
- ISACA resources"
